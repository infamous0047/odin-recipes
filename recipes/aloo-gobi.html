Aloo Gobi

    3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided ½ teaspoon cumin seed 1 small onion, quartered and sliced 2 serrano chile peppers, minced 1 teaspoon ginger paste 2 teaspoons ground coriander ½ teaspoon turmeric powder ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper ½ teaspoon garam masala ¼ teaspoon paprika 2 medium baking potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces 1 teaspoon salt ½ head cauliflower, cut into florets 2 teaspoons lemon juice

Steps

    Step 1

    Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add cumin seeds; cook until turn golden brown and begin to pop, a few seconds. Reduce heat to medium. Add onion; cook until lightly browned. Stir in serrano chiles and ginger paste; cook 1 minute. Season with coriander, turmeric, cayenne, garam masala, and paprika; cook until fragrant, 30 seconds.
